Answers To Your Home Cleaning Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What do Tidy Theory home cleaners clean?

Our cleaners use our proprietary 50-point home cleaning checklist to make sure that no dust or mess is spared in our thorough home cleans. Every surface, every room, every time.

How long will my home clean take?

That depends! Our cleaners typically take approximately 90 minutes for a one bedroom apartment. Time increases by roughly an hour for each additional bedroom. Homes with pets, or that haven't had a good clean in a while, can take longer.

How much will my home clean cost?

Our pricing system is transparent and based on the size of your home. You can use our Book Now tool to see your pricing breakdown and schedule your home clean in less than sixty seconds.

How often should I get my home cleaned?

We recommend a fortnightly clean to make sure that your home is kept in an ideal condition. However, some of our customers prefer weekly or monthly, depending on if they have kids, pets and the amount of their own time they're able to commit to cleaning.

Once I've booked can I change my time?

Yes! Our booking system is flexible and dynamic. Once you've confirmed your home clean you're able to change timing as often as you'd like.

Do I have to be at home when my cleaners arrive?

Not always! If you're able to leave a key in a safe space we can access your home in your absence. Our cleaners undergo police checks and extensive character references to make sure that only trustworthy cleaners are allowed in your home.

What suburbs do you clean?

Tidy Theory was born and raised in Sydney's Eastern Suburbs. From Darlinghurst to Bondi and Double Bay to Maroubra, we are focused on keeping Sydney's East sparkling clean. If you're unsure if your home is in our service area please reach out, we'd love to chat!
