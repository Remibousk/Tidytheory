The Tidy Theory: Why We Use a 50-Point Checklist

Most cleaning services "eye-ball" a room. They look for the obvious dust and move on. But at Tidy Theory, we believe that a premium clean is found in the details you don't see at first glance.

Consistency Requires a System

Our "Theory" is simple: consistency requires a system. Whether it's your first deep clean or your fiftieth recurring service, our team follows a rigorous 50-Point Checklist designed for high-end Sydney homes.

Beyond the Surface

We don't just wipe the counters; we clean under the appliances. We don't just vacuum the floors; we ensure the skirting boards are free of scuffs. From the top of the fridge to the chrome fixtures in your ensuite, our checklist ensures that no corner is left unturned.

It's the difference between a home that looks clean from across the room and one that's genuinely spotless when you run your finger along the windowsill.

The White-Glove Standard

By removing the guesswork, we provide a reliable, "white-glove" result every single time. Your cleaner doesn't decide what gets done based on how they're feeling that day, the system decides. And the system is thorough.

This is what separates a cleaning service from a cleaning system. And it's why our clients stay with us.
