How to Choose the Right Home Cleaning Service For You

Finding a cleaner in Sydney is easy; finding the right partner for your home is the real challenge. With so many options, from solo operators to giant franchises, how do you ensure you're getting a service that respects your home as much as you do?

When choosing a service for your Eastern Suburbs home, look for these three non-negotiables:

1. The Guarantee

What happens if something isn't right? A premium service should offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with the clean, they should come back and make it right, no questions asked, no awkward conversations. This tells you they stand behind their work and aren't just collecting payment.

2. Transparent, Flat-Rate Pricing

The old "hourly rate" model often leads to "slow-playing" the clock. Look for a service that offers flat rates based on the size of your home so you know exactly what you're paying upfront. No surprises. No creeping invoices. Just a clear number before the clean even starts.

3. Vetted Professionals

Don't be afraid to ask about their hiring process. Are the cleaners police-checked? Are they experienced? How selective is the company? At Tidy Theory, we believe trust is the foundation of a clean home. That's why only the top 2% of applicants make it onto our team.

What to Expect from a Premium Clean

When a Tidy Theory professional arrives, expect a seamless experience. We start with a walkthrough to note your priorities, follow our systematic 50-point checklist, and finish with a quality check. You should expect to walk into a home that doesn't just look clean, but feels renewed.
